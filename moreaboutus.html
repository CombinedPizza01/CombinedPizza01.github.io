MORE ABOUT US

Plain old pizza is a thing of the past, here at Pizza Development
we want to revolutionize the pizza industry. We have our research
teams working around the clock to make pizza better... New crust,
toppings, sauce, and so much more! We promise to make your pizza
more enjoyable. We are even teaming up with companies such Pizza
Hut, Dominoes, and even Papa John's! You too can help our studies by
becoming a memeber of the Pizza Society. With this payment of $5
a month you will get a free sample pizza every month and a hat to
let people know you love pizza just as much as us. Thank you for
supporting Pizza development!
